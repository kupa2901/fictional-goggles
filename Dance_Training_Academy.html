Training Schedule

Our carefully designed training schedule takes you from beginner to professional dancer in 12 months. Each phase builds on the skills learned in the previous one to ensure consistent and progressive improvement.

  • Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Foundation Building - Basics, rhythm, and coordination.
  • Phase 2 (Months 4-6): Intermediate Growth - Increased complexity, stamina, and musicality.
  • Phase 3 (Months 7-9): Advanced Mastery - High-speed execution and artistry.
  • Phase 4 (Months 10-12): Professional Performance - Mastery and endurance.
